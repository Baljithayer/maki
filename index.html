An unobtrusive point of interest icon set made especially for MapBox.

68 different symbols at 3 sizes each. 204 total icons and growing.

Uniquely crafted to make your TileMill maps beautiful and meaningful.

Designed pixel-by-pixel to look great at small sizes but scale up elegantly

We designed Maki specifically for TileMill with goal of creating an international, comprehensive, and stylistically unified point of interest icon set. Each symbol is drawn three times at different sizes to maximize crispness and readability. Maki symbols are based on international recognized symbols, following precedents set by AIGA and Isotype but preserving a unique look at feel.

Use Maki for everything from adding context to the base map of your mobile app to highlighting critical data on your disaster map.

Maki and MapBox give you more control

Maki icons are easy to use with MapBox. Just reference the icons in TileMill with Carto to include exactly as little or as much context as you want in your map. For detailed instructions, check out the documentation.

Open source and actively developed

Maki icons are licensed under BSD, which means you're free to use, modify, and share these icons. If you'd like to make requests or suggest changes, create an issue on Github or send us an E-mail.

View the public repository at github.com/mapbox/maki
